About Us

We believe skincare is a ritual of self-love and connection to nature. Our journey began with our founder, Dr. Jackie, who sought natural solutions for her own skin concerns after struggling with harsh chemical-based products. Inspired by ancestral remedies and the purity of nature, she crafted transformative formulations that quickly gained admiration from friends and family, sparking the birth of Pearls .

Rooted in authencity and sustainability, we create products using ethically sourced, natural ingredients free from harmful chemicals.Every formula is thoughtfully designed to nourish your skin while respecting the planet.

More than skincare, Pearls is a movement to inspire confidence and celebrate individually. Our mission is to empower you with clean beauty solutions tailored to your unique needs, helping you embrace your natural glow.
